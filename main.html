Ye Jawani Hai Diwani
Actors Quotes

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a 2013 Bollywood film directed by Ayan Mukerji, starring Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, and Kalki Koechlin. The film is a coming-of-age story about a young man who embarks on a journey to find meaning in his life.

Actors

Ranbir Kapoor as Bunny

Deepika Padukone as Naina

Aditya Roy Kapur as Avi

Kalki Koechlin as Aditi

"Zindagi ek safar hai, aur safar mein hi pyaar ho jaata hai." - Avi
